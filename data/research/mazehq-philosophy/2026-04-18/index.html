Our Philosophy - Maze

Our Philosophy

As an industry, security is in a strange place. We buy more tools than ever, yet trust between customers and vendors is at an all-time low. Too often, there is a lack of craft in the way we build security products, and a lack of respect in the way we market and sell them. Many security teams have had enough

We’re building Maze to be a different type of security company, based on a few core principles.
01
02
03

Obsess about product

We think security products can be a lot, lot better. We sweat the small details, care deeply about design, and seek to build something people really, really love.

Push boundaries with AI

AI is not just a marketing term for us – it’s the reason we exist and the thing we’re best at. We aim to always be at the edge of what’s possible, pushing the boundaries of what AI can do for security teams.

Put security teams first

Everything we do starts with what’s best for our customers. In product, sales, marketing and beyond we start by thinking about what security teams actually want and need, not what’s best for us.

Contribute to the community

The best thing about security is that we’re all (pretty much) on the same team. We seek to continuously contribute to making that team successful.

Build an exceptional team

Delivering an exceptional product and service requires an exceptional team. We hire from a wide global talent pool, hold ridiculously high standards, and seek to hire the very best wherever they are.
